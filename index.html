About Me

I am passionate about video games, starting with the GameCube when I was just five years old. In high school, I took a course on video game programming and after the first class I knew I had found my calling.

I recently graduated with a B.S in Computer Science with a concentration in Game Development at Fairleigh Dickinson University. In my time at FDU, I was tasked with creating multiple small games. Those projects helped give me a basic understanding of the work that goes into the development of video games. A big reason I decided to study game development was to create a product that millions could enjoy playing while helping them through tough times.
